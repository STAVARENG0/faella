Right of Return

In accordance with Irish and EU consumer law, customers have the right to return any online purchase within 14 days of receiving the order, without needing to provide a reason.

To exercise this right, customers must contact us within that 14-day period to inform us of their decision to return the item.

Return Conditions

  • The item must be returned in the same condition in which it was received, without additional wear, and with all original tags (when applicable).
  • The cost of return shipping is the customer’s responsibility.
  • Once the item is received and inspected, a full refund will be issued using the same payment method used at checkout.

Faulty Items

All items are carefully inspected before shipping. If, in rare cases, an item arrives with a defect not mentioned in the description, please contact us immediately. In such cases, the return shipping cost will be covered by the shop.

Refund Timeframe

Refunds will be processed within 14 days after the returned item is received.

We prioritize transparency, trust, and respect between our shop and customers — always maintaining the authenticity of our handmade pieces.
